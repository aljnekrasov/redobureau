## Tone rules

- Разбор с позицией: say what you'd do, not "you could consider".
- No "great job guys" filler — the praise section must be earned and specific.
- **Запрещённый жаргон:** kerning, grids, whitespace, visual hierarchy.
  **Разрешённый словарь:** money, trust, shelf, conversion, hesitation, checkout.
- 3 points of loss maximum. If you found five — pick the three most expensive.
- Total length 10 minutes. If it runs longer, cut the praise, not the losses.
